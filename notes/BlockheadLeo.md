# 2026-05-23

From Sophia lesson today, I have learnt that ERC-8004 and the x402 protocol act as the fundamental trust and payment layers for AI agents and machine-to-machine interactions. Together, they allow autonomous software agents to identify one another, verify trustworthiness, negotiate, and execute secure micropayments with zero human intervention.

Basically, ERC-8004 (The Identity & Reputation Layer) is an Ethereum standard serves as a universal "passport" or "ID card" for AI agents. It establishes a permanent on-chain identity for agents, tracking their reputation scores, validation records, and history.  
  
Whereas for x402 (The Payment Layer\], it is an HTTP-layer payment protocol that serves as the "wallet" for machines. It utilizes the traditional HTTP \\(402\\) (Payment Required) status code, allowing agents to request and receive payments directly on the web.
